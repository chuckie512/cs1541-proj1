Adding a 1-bit branch predictor over assuming a branch isn't taken gives us a
7% increase in CPI for the simple pipeline and a 7% increase in CPI for the
superscaler pipeline. Clearly there is an improvement in predicting correct
branches between assume not taken and 1-bit prediction. The majority of
improved predictions most likely comes from loops within the traces.

Changing from a single pipeline to two pipelines gave a 4% increase in CPI for
the assume not taken predictor, and a 4% increase in CPI for the 1-bit
predictor. This improvement likely comes from the issue two case.  The issuer
will not issue any worse than the single pipline, but in certain cases allows
two instrucitons to be calculated at the same time.
